Our Premium Wedding Halls

Royal Mahal offers a selection of exquisite wedding venues designed to make your special day truly memorable. Each hall is meticulously maintained and equipped with modern amenities.

Hall Features:

  • Elegant interiors with customizable decor options
  • Spacious seating capacity for 200-1000 guests
  • State-of-the-art sound and lighting systems
  • Premium catering services with customizable menus
  • Dedicated bridal suites and groom rooms
  • Ample parking facilities
  • Professional event coordination services

Booking Process:

Booking your wedding hall with Royal Mahal is simple:

  1. Select your preferred venue and date
  2. Choose your package and customization options
  3. Complete the booking with a 30% deposit
  4. Meet with our event coordinator to plan details
  5. Enjoy your perfect wedding day!

Packages:

We offer three comprehensive packages to suit every need:

  • Silver Package: Basic hall rental with standard decor
  • Gold Package: Includes premium decor, basic sound system, and standard catering
  • Platinum Package: Full-service package with premium amenities, custom decor, gourmet catering, and dedicated coordinator

Help Center

Find answers to common questions

How do I book a wedding hall?

Booking is simple! Visit our website, select your preferred venue and date, choose your package, and complete the payment process. You can also call our booking hotline for assistance.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations made more than 90 days before the event receive a full refund. Between 90-60 days, 50% refund. Less than 60 days, no refund but you can reschedule based on availability.

Can I customize the decor?

Absolutely! We offer various decor packages and also allow customizations. Our design team will work with you to create your perfect wedding theme.

Do you provide catering services?

Yes, we have in-house catering with customizable menus. We offer vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and special dietary options. You can also arrange external catering with prior notice.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI payments, net banking, and bank transfers. We also accept cash payments at our office.

Can I visit the venue before booking?

Certainly! We encourage venue visits. Please schedule an appointment through our website or by calling our office to arrange a viewing.
